B Spline.vi

 degree

is the degree of the spline. It must be less than the dimension of the points.

knots

is the knot vector for the spline. It's length must be equal to the length of the control points + degree + 1. If no knot is passed, a knot vector similar to that of [0, 1, ..., n] will be produced. To generate a knot vector that produces a clamped spline, use Generate Clamped Knot Vector.vi

weights

is the weights for the spline. It must be the same length as the control points.

error in
